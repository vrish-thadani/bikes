History of Bikes

Bikes have come a long way since their invention in the early 19th century.
From wooden prototypes to modern-day electric and racing bikes,
the evolution of biking reflects innovation, freedom, and lifestyle.
Today, bikes are used for transport, adventure, sport, and even sustainability.

Types of Bikes

There are several types of bikes, including mountain bikes, road bikes, cruisers, hybrids, and electric bikes.
Each type is designed for specific terrains and purposes, catering to a wide range of riders.

Famous Bike Brands

The biking world is dominated by iconic brands like Trek, Giant, Specialized, Yamaha, Harley-Davidson, and Honda.
These brands produce bikes for various categories from daily commuting to pro racing and adventure travel.

Technology & Features

Modern bikes come with features like gear shifting systems, GPS, hydraulic brakes, shock absorbers,
lightweight frames, and smart tracking. Electric bikes (e-bikes) are becoming increasingly popular for urban travel.

Future of Biking

The future of biking includes advancements in AI-powered e-bikes, solar charging, smart helmets,
and increased integration with mobile apps for performance and safety tracking.
Biking continues to play a role in eco-friendly and efficient transport worldwide.
